BARBIE

Showing Time: 1hr 26min - long

Age Restrition: PG 13


Barbie is a fantasy comedy film about an eccentric and individualistic doll.
She is exiled from Barbieland because of her imperfections.
She suffers a crisis that leads her to question her world and her existence.
When Barbieland is in danger, she returns with the knowledge that what makes her different also makes her stronger
