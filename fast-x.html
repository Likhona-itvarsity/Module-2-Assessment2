FAST X

Showing Time: 2hr 25min - long

Age Restrition: PG 13


Fast X is a movie about Dom Toretto and his family being targeted by the vengeful son of drug kingpin Hernan Reyes.

The son, Dante, wants to make Dom suffer by killing his family and loved ones because

Dante's father died due to Dom's actions in Fast Five.

The plot will take the family to different locationsaround the world, and Dom will have to ensure that his family and loved ones are safe from Dante's plan.
