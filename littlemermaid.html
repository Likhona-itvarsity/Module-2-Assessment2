LITTLE MERMAID

Showing Time: 2hr 15min - long

Age Restrition: PG


The Little Mermaid follows a mermaid princess Ariel who is fascinated with the human world

and makes a deal with a treacherous sea witch Ursula to trade her voice for human legs to impress Prince Eric,

whom she saved from a shipwreck before time runs out.


Characters

King Triton - Javier Bardem

Ursula - Melissa McCarthy

Eric - Jonah Hauer-King
